    Who We Are

    GIGO Delivery Services Private Limited is a dynamic startup based in Tamil Nadu, specializing in e-commerce express parcel delivery and value-added services. We are proud to be a leading provider in the region, with an extensive distribution network covering every PIN code across South India, including Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and Telangana.

    We offer end-to-end support for corporate clients who need more than just delivery. From dedicated account management and scheduled pickups to staffing and warehousing, we’re here to simplify your operations so you can focus on growth.

    With real-time tracking and dedicated customer support, we offer a seamless and transparent experience for both businesses and their customers. Our trained delivery professionals prioritize safe handling and punctual performance, helping you strengthen and maintain your brand’s trust.
